Connected Professional

Throughout my years of being in leadership in numerous schools, I have initiated and organized partnerships with the schools below. It has not only provided a valuable platform to enhance the professional development among our teams: it has also provided us with opportunities to evaluate and improve our current systems, and brought in new ideas to implement at my current school. These relationships have also extended into new marketing opportunities and given us a better insight of the directions in our industries.

I have independently and proactively sought and connected with elite international and private schools across Asia, China, Taiwan, Bangkok and Singapore, as well as taking our school into the Sister School Partnership scheme encouraged by the HKEDB.

Organizations I have worked closely with:
  • Chinese Middle School Sanya School (Sanya, China)
  • SheKou International School (ShenZhen, China)
  • Independent Schools Foundation (Hong Kong)
  • Harrow International School (Hong Kong)
  • Regents International School ( Pattaya, Thailand)
  • St. Andrews International School (Bangkok, Thailand)
  • KIS International School (Bangkok, Thailand)
  • Ministry of Education (Singapore)
  • Raffles Girls School (Singapore)
  • Anglo-Chinese School (Singapore)
  • Morrison Academy (Taichung, Taiwan)
  • I-Shou International School (Kaohsiung,Taiwan)
