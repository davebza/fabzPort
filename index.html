Innovative Leader

As an innovative leader in education, I am adept at looking into the situation and prioritizing the circumstances with honesty. I am aware of the selflessness I need to carry as innovation must be inclusive If you want to go fast, go alone; if you want to go far, go together. Despite all the cliche quotes of being a risk taker as a leader, I don’t agree to take a half-baked idea under the name of an “innovative spirit”. instead I take in the essence of being prepared to fail. Most of all, I pursue and value the trust of my most critical audience - the students. Without them the innovative will never come.

One of the most significant innovations in education over the last decade has been the use of technology. In the classroom, this has given us the promise of individualized learning to maximise student potential, while outside of the classroom we have the chance of revolutionizing our school’s operational efficiency. As a leader in education, I am constantly reminding myself to be engaged and equipped in the new technologies and be connected with the new models.
