Connected Professional

Throughout my years of being the leader of numerous schools. I have initiated and organized partnership with the schools below. It has not only providing a valuable platform to enhance the professional development among my team, providing opportunities to evaluate and improve the current system, bringing in new ideas to implement at my current school. It has also created new marketing opportunities and provided a better insight of the directions in our industries.

I have proactively seeked and connected with elite international and private schools across Asia, China, Taiwan, Bangkok and Singapore as well as being involved in the Sister School Partnership scheme encouraged by the HKEDB.

  • Overseas Chinese Middle School Sanya School (Sanya, China)
  • SheKou International School (ShenZhen, China)
